    What the Scorecard Measures

    The Financial Strength Scorecard is built around the four pillars I see make or break agent businesses. It's the same diagnostic I run on every new coaching client before we start working together — now available free.

    Pillar 1

    Structure & Protection

    Entity status, business vs. personal account separation, liability protection. Does your structure match your income level?

    Pillar 2

    Visibility & Numbers

    Do you have a real P&L? Do you know your net (not gross)? Do you review numbers monthly or quarterly?

    Pillar 3

    Cash Flow & Taxes

    Commission cash-flow system in place? Tax money set aside? CPA relationship beyond once-a-year filing?

    Pillar 4

    Wealth & Investing

    Is business profit being converted to assets? Any investment property? Net worth target? MREI-style plan?

    Why this matters: Real estate is a great income business and a terrible wealth business — if you only run it as a sales practice. The agents who reach 55 with a real balance sheet are the ones who treat themselves as a financially literate operator first, a salesperson second. This scorecard tells you which side of that line you're on right now.
