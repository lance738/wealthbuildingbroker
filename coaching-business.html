The MREI Framework, Translated

The Three Models — Applied to Your Business

The MREI was written about real estate, but the three core models work for every business. Here's how we adapt them.

01

Net Worth Model

"Net worth is the scoreboard."

We separate your business net worth from your personal net worth and track both. Most owners conflate the two — and end up with neither growing. We engineer both, deliberately.

02

Cash Flow Model

"Cash flow is the engine."

Net worth gets you free; cash flow keeps you free. We engineer monthly cash flow from your business and from outside assets so you're not 100% dependent on the business performing every month forever.

03

Lead Generation Model

"Leads don't find you. You find leads."

Every business runs on leads. We build three independent lead channels — so you're never one referral source away from collapse. Then we systematize them so they run without you in the room every day.

Four Stages of Wealth Building

Where You Are. Where You're Going.

Stage 1 — Think a Million

Mindset and goal-setting. Specific net worth target, specific date. The number you'll be embarrassed to tell your spouse — and then we work backward.

Stage 2 — Buy a Million

Acquisition. Both growing the business and routing profits into income-producing assets (real estate is the easiest, but not the only one).

Stage 3 — Own a Million

Optimization. Paying down debt, improving operations, building equity in both the business and the asset portfolio.

Stage 4 — Receive a Million

Income that runs without you. The business has either been sold, transitioned to a team, or generates passive distributions — and the asset portfolio pays you whether you work or not.

Now Part of Every Coaching Engagement

How to Actually Use AI to Grow Your Small Business

AI is rewriting what a small team can accomplish. Solo founders are running operations today that needed five people two years ago. But most owners are dabbling — letting AI tools become another shiny distraction instead of a real leverage point. In coaching, we work AI into the MREI framework you're already building, so it adds output without adding overhead.

Specific AI applications we'll work on together — adapted to your industry:

  • Customer service automation — AI-powered chat, voice, and email response systems that handle the easy 80% so your team can focus on the high-value 20%
  • Sales process & proposal generation — AI-drafted proposals, follow-ups, and discovery summaries that compress your sales cycle
  • Content marketing & SEO — building a content engine that consistently feeds your lead generation channels without a full-time marketer
  • Operations & SOP creation — turning the procedures locked in your head into documented systems so the business can run without you
  • Financial analysis — AI on top of QuickBooks/Xero data to surface cash flow patterns, margin leaks, and pricing opportunities most owners miss
  • HR & team management — AI-assisted job descriptions, interview prep, performance reviews, and policy drafting
  • Review & reputation management — automated response drafts to Google/Yelp/social reviews that protect your brand 24/7
  • Custom GPTs / Claude Projects for your business — building a private AI assistant trained on your services, your pricing, your standards — so anyone on the team gets consistent answers without bothering you
  • Workflow automation — connecting AI to your CRM, billing, calendar, and task systems so the busywork runs itself
The principle: AI is a multiplier, not a replacement. If you have no system, AI just helps you generate more chaos faster. If you have a system, AI is the leverage that turns a small business into one that competes with companies twice your size. We build the system (MREI framework) first — then layer AI on top.

I personally use AI tools every day in my brokerage and investing work. In coaching, I'll show you which tools, which prompts, and which integrations are actually moving the needle right now — adapted to your specific business and industry.
