Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Our Commitment

Wealth Building Broker is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, including people with disabilities. We work to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, the standard cited by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), the California Unruh Civil Rights Act, and similar regulations.

Measures We Take

To make this site accessible, we:

  • Use semantic HTML with proper headings, landmarks, and ARIA labels
  • Provide alt text for meaningful images
  • Maintain sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • Ensure pages are fully keyboard-navigable (tab, enter, space)
  • Use form labels associated with their inputs
  • Make the site responsive across screen sizes and zoom levels (up to 200%)
  • Avoid auto-playing audio, flashing content, and other accessibility hazards
  • Allow text resizing through browser controls without breaking layouts

Compatibility

The site is designed to work with:

  • Recent versions of major browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
  • Screen readers including VoiceOver (Mac/iOS), NVDA, and JAWS
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Operating-system zoom and high-contrast modes

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some content may not yet meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA:

  • PDF resources — Some downloadable PDFs may not be fully tagged for screen reader compatibility. If you need an accessible version of any PDF, contact us and we will provide one within a reasonable time.
  • Third-party embedded content — Videos hosted on YouTube and content from other third-party services (Instagram, Amazon) follow those platforms' accessibility standards, which we do not control.
  • Calculators — Our calculators rely on standard form inputs but complex result displays may need refinement for older screen reader versions.

We continue to improve accessibility on an ongoing basis.

Need Help or Have a Concern?

If you experience an accessibility barrier on this site, or if you need information or services in an alternative format, please contact us. We will work to address your concern as quickly as possible.
