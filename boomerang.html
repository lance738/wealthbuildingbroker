The math nobody runs

NAR says most sellers would use their agent again — but only a small fraction actually do. The difference isn't loyalty. It's follow-up. Boomerang closes that gap systematically.

How It Works

Three Steps. Zero New Prospecting.

Boomerang doesn't find strangers. It re-activates the database you already earned.

📥

1. Load Your Book

Import your closed transactions and sphere — from your CRM export or a simple spreadsheet. Boomerang builds a profile of every relationship: when they bought, what they bought, what it's likely worth now.

🎯

2. Score the Signals

Each contact gets a likelihood-to-transact score from tenure in home, equity position, life-stage signals, and engagement history — so you know who to call, not just that you "should call your database."

🪃

3. Work the Short List

A weekly short list of the highest-probability contacts with a suggested reason to reach out. Ten focused conversations beat a hundred generic touches.
